Alpha GPC

Alpha‑GPC (L‑alpha‑glycerylphosphorylcholine) is a choline source commonly used in nootropic and sport‑nutrition contexts.

At‑a‑glance
  • Often found in 150–300 mg capsules per serving (varies by brand).
  • Typically taken once or twice daily per label instructions.
  • May be combined with other choline donors in complex formulas.

Typical label directions

Always follow the specific brand label. Serving sizes and cautions vary by product.

Safety notes

People with mood disorders, on prescription medicines, or with chronic conditions should consult a qualified clinician before use.

Good to know

This page is for general information only and is not medical advice. If you have health conditions, are pregnant, or take medicines, seek professional guidance.
